welcome

Welcome to my log. Here I’ll be posting many links to sites with a record of my thoughts and comments on such articles, with occasional long form articles (which I predict I will never actually get around to). In a way, this is just a tumblr replacement. However, I think there was merit in learning how to set up a microblog with Hugo, so this was absolutely not a waste of time.

/me stops rationalising

It’s not called a blog because in the usual sense of the word, that implies longer form articles. It is not called “microblog” as I think that’s a mouthful to type. Hence, it is called a log, in the sense of a computer log, just more human.

Anyways, have fun reading this log, I guess.
